Your privacy on Accenture's site

The primary purpose of the mySupplier Setup site is to have a centralized supplier registration portal for Accenture to manage supplier relations and track prequalification, on-boarding and proactive communications.

We want you to feel secure when visiting our site and are committed to maintaining your privacy when doing so. This policy explains how any personal data we may collect from or about you through this site will be processed and protected by Accenture. Any changes we may make to our privacy policy in the future will be posted on this page.

What information do we gather?

Accenture collects information at its sites in two ways: (1) directly (for example, when you register as an Accenture Supplier, or enter information for facilitating invoicing and payment); and (2) indirectly (for example, through our site's technology like cookie and piwik).

We may collect and process the following information:

  • Information that you provide by filling in forms on our site. This includes registering to use the site, facilitating invoicing and payment or requesting further information. Pages that collect this type of information may provide further information as to why your data is needed and how it will be used.

  • Any postings, comments or other content that you upload or post to an Accenture site.

  • Details of your visits to our site and resources you access (including but not limited to, traffic data, location data, weblogs and other communication data). Please see the Cookies section below for more information.

  • Information entered by the supplier, including supplier inclusion and diversity information (e.g. SMEs).

When this information relates to or identifies you, it is "personal data".

Except for certain information that is required by law, your decision to provide any personal data to us is voluntary. You will therefore not be subject to adverse consequences if you do not provide personal data. However, please note that if you do not provide certain information, we may not be able to accomplish some or all of the purposes outlined in this privacy statement, and you may not be able to use the mySupplier Setup site where it requires the use of such personal data.

If you choose to provide us with unsolicited sensitive personal data, we will only use such data subject to applicable law as described in this privacy statement.
